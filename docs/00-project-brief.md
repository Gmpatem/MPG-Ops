# Project Brief: MPG Ops

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** MPG Ops  
**Type:** Mobile-first SaaS Platform  
**Target Users:** Service business owners (salons, barbershops, spas)

---

## Problem Statement

Service businesses currently rely on notebooks and manual systems, leading to:

- **Missed bookings** — No centralized scheduling system
- **Poor customer tracking** — Customer history scattered or non-existent
- **Unclear daily revenue** — No real-time financial visibility
- **Operational inefficiency** — Time wasted on manual record-keeping

---

## Solution

MPG Ops provides a unified digital platform for:
- Managing appointments and bookings
- Tracking customers and their service history
- Recording payments and monitoring revenue
- Getting daily business summaries

---

## Target Users

### Primary User: Business Owner
- Small to medium service business owner
- Manages day-to-day operations
- Needs quick, mobile-friendly access
- Wants clear financial visibility

### Business Types Supported
| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| `salon` | Hair salons, beauty salons |
| `barbershop` | Men's grooming, barber services |
| `spa` | Wellness spas, massage therapy |

---

## Success Criteria

1. **Bookings** — Zero missed appointments due to system failure
2. **Customer Management** — Complete customer profiles accessible in < 3 seconds
3. **Revenue Tracking** — Real-time daily revenue visibility
4. **Mobile Experience** — Full functionality on mobile devices
5. **Setup Time** — New business onboarded in < 10 minutes

---

## Non-Goals (Out of Scope)

The following are **explicitly excluded** from MVP:

- Payroll management
- Inventory/stock tracking
- Customer loyalty programs
- Marketing tools (SMS, email campaigns)
- Multi-branch support
- Advanced analytics/reports
- Staff scheduling/shift management
- Online booking for customers (external)

---

## Core Principles

1. **Mobile-First** — Design for mobile, adapt for desktop
2. **Single Codebase** — One system for all business types
3. **Speed Over Features** — Fast, reliable core features over breadth
4. **Progressive Disclosure** — Simple UI that reveals complexity as needed
